About MMC

Meyer Management Consulting N.V. (MMC) is a Suriname-based firm committed to helping organizations to successfully navigate the Surinamese business landscape. With multiple footprints in Suriname, the Netherlands and the wider Caribbean, we specialize in market entry, corporate structuring, and business expansion, serving as the strategic bridge between local ambitions and international opportunities.

Founded in 2019, MMC was created to support organizations navigating change, expansion, and transformation—bridging local relevance with global standards. We specialize in advancing business development strategies tailored to Suriname’s evolving economic landscape.

With deep roots in strategic business consulting, MMC delivers tailor-made solutions grounded in implementation excellence, capacity building, and sustainable partnerships. We enable growth with impact—bridging local talent and international opportunity.

Our clients benefit from a one-stop business service offering, ensuring smooth operations from formation to growth—with a focus on compliance, financial integrity, and strategic partnerships.
